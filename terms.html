E.U. GENERAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATION

HELIX is investing time and energy to comply with the EU GENERAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATION (“GDPR”) as set forth by the Council of the European Union and European Parliament, which becomes enforceable May 25, 2018, regarding the collection, use, transmission, processing, and retention of personal identifiable data of natural persons from European Union member countries. The GDPR is meant to strengthen the security and protection of personal data and to harmonize data protection laws throughout the EU.

What this means is that we at HELIX are committed to protecting your data in a secure and transparent manner. HELIX processes, uses, and exchanges personal data for our legitimate business purposes only in the limited uses described herein.

The HELIX Platform only requires basic personal information from our users, such as name and email address, to facilitate user management, usage monitoring and provide our Services. Such data is stored in the HELIX Platform infrastructure and may in the future be shared with limited external vendors to the extent necessary to provide HELIX’s Services. HELIX does not use personal information for any purpose outside of providing the Services as set forth in this Privacy Policy.
